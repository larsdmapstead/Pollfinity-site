About Pollfinity

A Different Kind of Polling Organization

Pollfinity Research LLC was founded in 2026 with a single mandate: produce rigorous, independent public opinion research without the sponsorship conflicts, partisan skew, or methodological opacity that plague much of the modern polling industry.

We use AI-assisted automation to field surveys faster and more cost-effectively than traditional telephone operations, while maintaining the statistical standards — proper sampling frames, demographic weighting, transparent documentation — that institutional credibility demands. Every poll we publish includes full methodology, field dates, sample characteristics, and weighting procedures.

We do not accept funding from campaigns, PACs, parties, or advocacy organizations. Our work is entirely self-funded and conducted in the public interest.
